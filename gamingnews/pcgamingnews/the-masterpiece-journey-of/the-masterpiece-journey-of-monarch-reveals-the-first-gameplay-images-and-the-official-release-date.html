The masterpiece Journey of Monarch reveals the first gameplay images and the official release date.

Monday, Sept. 30, 19:46h
By TN Agency

The latest and highly anticipated game from NCSOFT, Journey of Monarch, has revealed a gameplay trailer along with its official release date.

Journey of Monarch is currently the hottest title from the renowned game developer NCSOFT. The game draws its inspiration from the familiar setting and characters of the Lineage IP, developed and published by NCSOFT itself.

In the game Journey of Monarch, players will accompany the character Monarch, one of the most popular classes from the Lineage IP. Players' mission is to assemble a powerful team to embark on a challenging adventure and explore a fantasy world from a completely new perspective that you've never experienced before.

Contrary to what many gamers might expect, the mobile game Journey of Monarch is not an action RPG but a classic role-playing game inspired by the famous DnD series. Players will lead their team into thrilling turn-based battles with a unique dice-rolling mechanism. Now, both normal attacks and character skills incorporate elements of chance, making the intense battles even more exciting and engaging than ever before.

It is still unclear how many characters will be available in Journey of Monarch, but from the images and trailers released, the game features a variety of characters from different fantasy races, such as Humans, Elves, Dwarves, and Skeletons. Notably, the characters are designed using advanced 3D graphics powered by Unreal Engine 5, creating a mystical and vibrant world for players to explore and adventure in.

In addition, Journey of Monarch offers a full range of PvE and PvP activities with engaging gameplay for players to enjoy. From dungeon raids featuring gigantic bosses to thrilling arena battles, everything contributes to making this new mobile title from NCSOFT truly exceptional.
